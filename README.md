\# Electricity Calculator (PHP)



A vanilla PHP app to calculate:

\- Power (kW)

\- Energy usage (kWh) from hour 1 to 24

\- Total electricity cost (RM) based on rate (sen/kWh)



\## Formula

\- Power (W) = Voltage (V) × Current (A)

\- Power (kW) = Power (W) / 1000

\- Energy (kWh) = Power (kW) × Hour

\- Total (RM) = Energy (kWh) × (rate sen/kWh ÷ 100)
